===============================================================================
                📌 REMINDER: DYNAMIC NAVIGATION FILTERING BY ROLE
===============================================================================

Goal: Filter the top navigation bar items (both on desktop and mobile viewports)
dynamically based on the user's logged-in systemRole and permissions.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. TARGETED TABS FOR RECRUITERS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Recruiters should ONLY see:
- Dashboard (tailored Recruiter metrics, pipeline funnel)
- Job Posting (to view available requisitions to source candidates)
- Applicants (All Candidates, Pipeline, Resume Search, Interviews)
- Talent Bench (internal candidate bench hotlist)
- Mass Mail / Campaigns (move "Email" out of the "More" dropdown into the primary navbar)

Recruiters should NOT see:
- Clients (contracts, agreements, invoicing details)
- Vendors (vendor list, vendor portal)
- Onboarding (new hires, HR paperwork, background check status)
- Placements (timesheets, expenses, closed margins)
- Reports (company revenue metrics)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. TARGETED TABS FOR ACCOUNT MANAGERS (BDMs)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Account Managers / BDMs should see:
- Dashboard (Job requirement metrics, high priority placements)
- Job Posting (Create and manage client job requirements)
- Applicants (Review candidate submissions)
- Clients (Add new clients, agreements, contacts)
- Placements (View placements, approve timesheets / expenses)
- Reports (Basic closure ratios, submittal metrics)

Account Managers should NOT see:
- Vendors
- Onboarding

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
